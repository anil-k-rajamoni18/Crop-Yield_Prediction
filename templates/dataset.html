Yield Predictor Context

This dataset is originally collected from kaggle. The objective of the dataset is to diagnostically predict crop yield, based on PH value of soil, temparature,and crop included in the dataset. Several constraints were placed on the selection of these instances from a larger data.


Crop Sequence Predictor dataset collected from the following websites:

1.www.quora.com 2.www.beansandherbs.com 3.www.homeguide.com 4.www.pininterest.com 5.www.intechpen.com

Content

The datasets consists of several medical predictor variables and one target variable, Outcome. Predictor variables includes the PH of the soil, temparature, and crop.

Inspiration

Can you build a machine learning model to accurately predict whether or not the patients in the dataset have diabetes or not?
